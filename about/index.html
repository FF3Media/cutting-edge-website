Meet Musa Fitzgerald

"My name is Musa Fitzgerald, and I run Cutting Edge Lawn Care and Snow Removal. I've been cutting grass and maintaining properties for over 4 years, and I take a lot of pride in doing it right."

I'm new to the Kanata area, but I'm not new to this work. Before my family moved to Kanata last spring, I'd built a strong reputation in the West Island community. Clients knew they could count on me to show up on time, do clean work, and be easy to reach. That's the same standard I'm bringing to Kanata and the greater Ottawa area.
