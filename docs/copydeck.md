## FAQ Page

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We are based in Kanata and serve Stittsville, Barrhaven, Nepean, Orleans, and the greater Ottawa area.

**Q: How do I get a quote?**
A: Call us at (613) 700-6377 or fill out our online quote form. We respond within 24 hours.

**Q: Do you offer seasonal contracts?**
A: Yes. We offer full-season contracts for both lawn care and snow removal. This locks in your rate and guarantees your spot on our schedule.

**Q: What happens if you miss a scheduled visit?**
A: We do not miss visits. But if weather or an emergency causes a delay, we communicate proactively and make it up within 24 hours.

**Q: Are you insured?**
A: Yes. We carry full commercial liability insurance. Proof of insurance available on request.

**Q: When does snow removal service start?**
A: Our snow removal season runs from November through April. We recommend locking in your contract by October to guarantee your spot.

**Q: Do you salt driveways and walkways?**
A: Yes. Salting and sanding are available as add-ons or included in seasonal contracts.

**Q: How often do you mow?**
A: Weekly service is standard. Bi-weekly is available but we recommend weekly for the best results.

**Q: Can I cancel my contract?**
A: Yes. We offer flexible cancellation terms. No long-term lock-in. We keep clients by doing good work, not by trapping them in contracts.

**Q: Do you do commercial properties?**
A: Yes. We handle commercial snow removal and lawn maintenance for businesses across Ottawa.
