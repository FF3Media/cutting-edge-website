# Project Summary: Cutting Edge Lawn Care and Snow Removal

## Business Overview
Local service business operating in Kanata, Ontario (serving greater Ottawa). Dual-season model: lawn care (spring-fall) and snow removal (winter). Primary revenue driver is inbound lead generation via phone calls and quote requests.

## Primary Objective
Build a conversion-optimized, SEO-driven website that generates inbound leads from homeowners in Kanata and surrounding Ottawa suburbs. Every page is designed to drive two actions: Call Now or Request a Quote.

## Target Audience
- Homeowners in Kanata, Stittsville, Barrhaven, Nepean, Orleans, and greater Ottawa
- Middle to upper-income households ($80K-$200K+)
- Busy professionals and families who value reliability over price
- Property owners tired of inconsistent or unreliable providers

## Competitive Analysis Summary
Local competitors typically suffer from: outdated website designs, weak or missing calls-to-action, no social proof, poor mobile experience, no clear service area definition, and generic copywriting. Our site exploits every one of these gaps with: modern responsive design, persistent CTAs, trust signals on every page, mobile-first architecture, dedicated service area pages, and specific benefit-driven copy.

## Conversion Strategy
1. **Persistent CTA exposure**: Sticky header with phone number + quote button on every page
2. **Mobile-first click-to-call**: Phone number is tappable on all mobile devices
3. **Quote form accessibility**: Form embedded on homepage, contact page, and available via CTA from every page
4. **Trust cascade**: Reviews, years of experience, and local credibility appear above the fold
5. **Urgency and specificity**: Copy focuses on reliability, consistency, and local accountability
6. **SEO capture**: Location-specific pages target high-intent local search queries

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Architecture**: Static site generation (SSG) for maximum performance
- **SEO**: Built-in meta tags, Open Graph, structured data (JSON-LD)
- **Forms**: Client-side form with configurable backend endpoint
- **Deployment-ready**: Optimized for Vercel, Netlify, or any static host
