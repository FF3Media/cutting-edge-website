# Sitemap: Cutting Edge Lawn Care and Snow Removal

```

/snow-removal               → Snow Removal Services
/service-areas              → Service Areas (hub page)
/service-areas/kanata       → Kanata (primary)

```

## Page Hierarchy

### Primary Navigation
1. Home
2. Services (dropdown)
   - Lawn Care
   - Snow Removal
3. Service Areas
4. About Us
5. Contact

### Footer Navigation
- All primary pages
- FAQ
- Individual service area links
- Contact information
- Privacy Policy (placeholder)

## Internal Linking Strategy
- Homepage links to both service pages and primary service areas
- Each service page links to relevant service areas and contact
- Service area pages link to both services and contact
- Every page has persistent header CTA and footer contact info
- FAQ links contextually to service and contact pages

## SEO URL Structure
- Clean, keyword-rich slugs
- Location pages nested under /service-areas/ for topical authority
- Service pages at root level for maximum SEO weight
